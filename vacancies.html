  • Senior Engineer - UI/UX UI/UX

    Job Description

    We are looking for an experienced Senior UI/UX Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate should have a passion for creating beautiful, intuitive, and user-friendly interfaces. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing UI/UX solutions that meet the needs of our users while supporting the overall strategy of our organization.

    Key Responsibilities

    • Develop and implement user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) solutions that align with project requirements and meet user needs. Work collaboratively with cross-functional teams, including designers, product managers, developers, and stakeholders, to ensure timely delivery of high-quality solutions.
    • Conduct user research and gather feedback to continuously improve UI/UX solutions
    • Develop wireframes, prototypes, and mockups to communicate design ideas and facilitate testing
    • Develop and maintain design systems and style guides to ensure consistency across all digital touchpoints
    • Stay up-to-date with industry trends, best practices, and emerging technologies related to UI/UX design and development
    • Mentor junior designers and developers, providing guidance and feedback to ensure they meet project requirements and develop their skills

    Key Qualifications

    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
    • 4+ years of experience designing and developing UI/UX solutions for web and mobile applications
    • Strong proficiency in HTML 5, CSS 3, SASS, JavaScript, CSS frameworks such as Bootstrap and other relevant technologies
    • Experience using design tools such as Sketch, Figma, or similar
    • Experience in Angular 5, React JS, and a portfolio of UI/UX design is an added advantage, along with hands-on experience in Node.js and GIT version control
    • Knowledge of user research methodologies and tools, such as user interviews, usability testing, and A/B testing
    • Experience developing and maintaining design systems and style guides
    • Strong attention to detail and ability to produce high-quality work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
    • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
    • Experience mentoring and leading a team of junior designers and developers

    Benefits

    • Competitive salary and benefits package
    • Opportunity to work on exciting projects and cutting-edge technologies
    • Dynamic and collaborative work environment
    • Opportunity for career growth and development
